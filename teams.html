The drink contains caffeine, taurine, B vitamins, and sugar to boost energy and alertness.

Ferrari, an iconic Italian car manufacturer, is renowned for its strong presence in Formula 1. The Scuderia Ferrari team, established in 1929, is the oldest and most successful in F1 history, boasting numerous Constructors' and Drivers' Championships. Known for their distinctive red cars, Ferrari represents excellence and passion in motorsport.
